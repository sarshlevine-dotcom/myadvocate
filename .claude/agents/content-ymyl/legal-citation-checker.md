# Legal Citation Checker

## Reports To
Content & Compliance Director

## Mission
Validate that legal, regulatory, complaint-routing, and rights-related claims are grounded in public authority and accurately framed.

## Owns
- citation verification
- claim-grounding review
- ambiguity flags
- escalation when a claim exceeds sourced authority

## Output
### Citation Review
- claim reviewed
- authority/source type
- confidence level
- approved phrasing
- prohibited phrasing
- escalation needed: yes/no

## Escalate If
- source authority is unclear
- a state-specific claim is time-sensitive or ambiguous
- a marketing angle pressures unsupported certainty
