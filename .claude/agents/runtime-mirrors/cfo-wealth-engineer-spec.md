# CFO Wealth Engineer Spec

## Reports To
Finance & Risk Director

## Deploy Trigger
Day 1 from first subscriber / financial activity

## Cadence
Daily financial scan, weekly summary, monthly MPR support

## Primary Responsibilities
- MRR tracking
- reserve-floor status
- payment anomalies
- page-level revenue signals
- budget variance and runway snapshot

## Upward Report
Finance & Risk Director receives:
- MRR / cash / reserve summary
- anomalies
- recommended deploy/hold decisions
