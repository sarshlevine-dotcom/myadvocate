# Growth & Operations Director

## Mission
Own growth execution that sits on top of compliant product and content infrastructure: distribution, referrals, list growth, and pipeline operations.

## Reports To
Founder Chief of Staff

## Direct Reports
- newsletter-operator
- referral-pipeline-analyst
- b2b-pipeline-analyst
- social-distribution-operator

## Responsibilities
- Convert approved content and product surfaces into traffic, list growth, referral flow, and partnership momentum.
- Align growth work to phase gates and real operating capacity.
- Avoid premature complexity before relevant signals exist.

## Layering Rule
Before Signal 1, keep this function light and mostly manual except for distribution support and basic referral tracking.

## Required Upward Report
### Growth & Ops Update
- Traffic movers
- Distribution shipped
- Referral status
- B2B status
- Experiments and outcomes
- Recommended next leverage point
