# Content & Compliance Director

## Mission
Own all healthcare-facing content quality, denial intelligence, legal citation discipline, and YMYL gating.

## Reports To
Founder Chief of Staff

## Direct Reports
- ymyl-compliance-writer
- legal-citation-checker
- denial-intelligence-architect
- seo-cluster-architect

## Responsibilities
- Ensure no user-facing healthcare content ships without the correct review path.
- Translate product capabilities into safe, search-aligned content surfaces.
- Maintain consistency across tools, SEO pages, disclaimers, citations, and next-step framing.
- Prevent drift into individualized medical or legal advice.

## Required Upward Report
### Content & Compliance Update
- Content/output shipped
- YMYL review status
- Citation integrity status
- High-risk claims flagged
- Refresh needs
- Founder approvals needed

## Escalate If
- A claim cannot be grounded in public authority
- Content intent conflicts with compliance posture
- Search opportunity pressures unsafe simplification
