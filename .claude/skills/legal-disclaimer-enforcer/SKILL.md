## Purpose

Append the required legal disclaimer to all MyAdvocate-generated documents. This is a mandatory compliance step — no document leaves the system without it.

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Check document output
Receive the generated document from the calling skill. Verify the document is complete before appending.

### Step 2 — Insert disclaimer
Append the following disclaimer at the bottom of every generated document, separated by a horizontal rule:

---

**Legal Disclaimer**

This document was generated by MyAdvocate, an AI-powered patient advocacy tool. The information provided is for general informational and advocacy purposes only and does not constitute legal, medical, or professional advice.

MyAdvocate is not a law firm, healthcare provider, or licensed insurance professional. Nothing in this document should be construed as creating an attorney-client, physician-patient, or other professional relationship.

Healthcare laws and insurance regulations vary by state and plan type. You should consult a licensed attorney, healthcare professional, or insurance specialist for advice specific to your situation.

By using MyAdvocate, you agree that Sarsh Levine dba MyAdvocate is not liable for any outcomes resulting from the use of documents generated by this tool.

For help with this document, contact: [support contact]

---

### Step 3 — Return compliant document
Return the document with the disclaimer appended. Signal to `document-quality-checker` that the disclaimer has been inserted.

## Notes

- The disclaimer text is authoritative — do not paraphrase or shorten it
- If the document already contains a disclaimer (e.g., from a prior run), do not duplicate it
- The disclaimer lives in `src/lib/disclaimer.ts` — any changes to the legal text require founder review
