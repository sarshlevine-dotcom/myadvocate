## Content Quality Checklist (Every Page, Every Wave)

Before any page enters the human review queue, it must pass the 5-layer automated EEAT safety stack (MA-EEAT-001 §8.1):

1. **Schema validation** — required structured data present
2. **Citation check** — all stats have a source reference; KFF stat has "through 2023" date reference
3. **Forbidden claims check** — none of the forbidden determinations (litigation, legal guarantees, outcome predictions, settlement estimates)
4. **Disclaimer presence** — standard MyAdvocate disclaimer appended
5. **Tier routing check** — Haiku vs Sonnet model correctly assigned; Sonnet only if document upload present

After automated checks pass, route to human review queue (review_level in content_pages.json: founder_review or clinical_review_needed).

Clinical review (LPN/LVN reviewer confirmation) required for: page_0001 (CO-50), page_0006 (How to Appeal pillar), page_0008 (Medical Necessity Hub), page_0019 (CO-151).
